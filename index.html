Meet the Author

Cierra U. Malone is an educator and entrepreneur born and raised in Cleveland, Ohio. She is a graduate of Cleveland City Schools and is dedicated to empowering individuals through education and leadership. She aspires to establish her own charter school, where she can create a learning environment that fosters academic excellence, innovation, and equity for all students.
